# Overview

![Fermah](/images/overview_banner.png)

Fermah is a universal proof market that connects two sides:

- **Supply side:** GPU machines operated by <GlossaryTooltip>EigenLayer</GlossaryTooltip> Operators
- **Demand side:** Any entity requiring zero-knowledge proofs

The Fermah <GlossaryTooltip>Matchmaker</GlossaryTooltip> aligns supply and demand, ensuring efficient allocation of resources while maintaining competitive pricing.

Through **Intelligent Orchestration**, Fermah optimizes machine utilization. Its **Lean Mechanism Design** reduces proof generation costs. And with **Universal Proving**, it works across any proof system, chain, or VM.

![Fermah Architecture](/images/overview_image.gif)

Fermah supports <GlossaryTooltip>zkVM</GlossaryTooltip>s, <GlossaryTooltip>zkEVM</GlossaryTooltip>s, and <GlossaryTooltip>Groth16</GlossaryTooltip> while maintaining credible neutrality.

The platform is optimized for cheap, fast, and reliable ZK-proof generation for diverse cryptographic applications.
